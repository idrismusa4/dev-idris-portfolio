In addition to my technical expertise, I am also skilled in working collaboratively with others and value attention to detail in all aspects of my work. Overall, I am a motivated and curious individual who is always eager to take on new challenges and opportunities.

With a strong foundation in programming and web development, I was able to successfully transition into this new area of expertise.

Looking back on my journey, I am grateful for the diverse set of skills and experiences that I have acquired along the way. From web development to cybersecurity, each step has helped me to develop a deeper understanding of the tech industry and how it can be used to shape the future.
